## Goal

Draft a short spoken objection response the rep can edit before using live.

## Response Shape

- Use a clear spoken flow: acknowledge the concern, ground the response in one evidence-backed fact, and land on a low-pressure next step.
- Return one tight paragraph only.
- Do not use headings, bullets, or labels like "Acknowledge", "Evidence", or "Bridge".

## Max Length

- Maximum 120 words

## Stakeholder-Specific Guidance

- For treating oncologists:
  Focus on patient workflow, biomarker relevance, turnaround, and practical next steps.
- For lab directors or path leaders:
  Focus on routing, specimen workflow, pricing context, reporting usability, and institutional rollout.
- For medical directors:
  Focus on program workflow, pilot language, adoption path, and account-level impact.

## Output Standard

- Must use the actual CRM note and tagged objection context above.
- Must mention the specific concern raised, not a generic placeholder concern.
- Must include at least one evidence-backed fact when available.
- Must end with a clear next ask aligned to the suggested next step.
- Must sound conversational, not legalistic or promotional.
