- Why now: {{why_now}}

## Goal

Draft a 30-second meeting opener the rep can use as a starting point.

## Tailoring Rules

- Tailor by specialty:
  Bring forward the most relevant Tempus workflow for lung, GI, breast, heme, pathology, hereditary, or rare-tumor use cases.
- Tailor by relationship:
  New prospects need a clean introduction; warm accounts need a sharper next step; champions should get expansion language.
- Tailor by objection history:
  If the CRM history shows a recurring concern, address it naturally instead of ignoring it.
- Tailor by recent publication:
  If a publication is available, use it as a lightweight credibility bridge when relevant.

## Output Requirements

- Use the context above directly; do not ask for more account details.
- Use one concrete, evidence-backed Tempus fact when available.
- Mention why this conversation matters now.
- End with a low-pressure next ask.
- Keep it sounding like a field rep, not a polished marketing asset.

## Length

- Aim for 80-110 words
